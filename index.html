    About Us

    About US

    Rondo is a modern radio show that is produced and presented in a way for people to see the sport in different perspective By using Data analytics to analyze games, By Using Music to express the emotions, By using History to understand The background In a short precise fun 1 hour limit.

    our goal

    The goals of our radio sport program are to entertain, inform, and engage our audience with captivating sports content. We strive to deliver up-to-date news, insightful analysis, and lively commentary on all the latest sporting events, while also fostering a sense of community among sports enthusiasts.

    Programs

    Match Review

    Rondo Match Review offers a unique perspective on the latest matches played each week, providing in-depth analysis and football insights from various angles. It's a platform where we dissect the week's games, offering a fresh take on each match and unraveling the nuances of football in an engaging and informative manner.

    Rondo Scout

    Rondo Scout is all about evaluating and boosting athletes' mental, physical, and social skills to help them excel both on and off the field

    Rondo Rythm

    Rondo Rhythm is a program where we blend music with sports, exploring the tunes that have made an impact on the sporting world. We delve into a variety of music genres that resonate with sports enthusiasts, creating a harmonious fusion that energizes and uplifts.

    Rondo History

    In Rondo History, we delve into the fascinating narratives of historical moments in sports, providing insights and perspectives that are both informative and thought-provoking. Our goal is to uncover the untold stories behind the events, guiding our audience towards a deeper understanding of the significance and impact of these milestones. Through our historical sports programs, we aim to engage and enlighten our listeners, offering a journey through time that enriches their appreciation of the sports they love.

    Rondo Fun Facts

    Rondo Fun Fact is a program where we share amusing and captivating events from football history, providing a delightful and entertaining experience for our audience.

    Rondo Page

    The Rondo page offers a unique program where captivating narrations of sports-related books come to life, immersing audiences in the thrilling world of athletic literature.
